Why we exist

Young people deserve room to explore before they are expected to decide.

Questions about careers, education, volunteering, and the future can arrive before a young person has had enough exposure to answer them. Navio Pathways exists to make that exploration more practical, welcoming, and connected to real community experiences.

We help youth move from uncertainty toward informed next steps-without promising a single “right” path or a guaranteed outcome.

How it works

A clearer path starts with one useful next step.

  1. 01

    Explore

    Start with interests, questions, strengths, and options-not pressure to have everything decided.

  2. 02

    Participate

    Join an available activity, use a resource, attend an event, or connect through a community partner.

  3. 03

    Build experience

    Practise skills, reflect on what fits, and learn from people and situations beyond the classroom.

  4. 04

    Move forward

    Choose a practical next action with greater awareness, confidence, and direction.
