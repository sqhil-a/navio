# Navio

**Career direction, made clear.**

Navio is a student-focused career planning app designed to help students explore career options, understand their strengths, build a roadmap, track progress, and take clear next steps toward their future.

Many students know they want to do something meaningful, but they are unsure where to start. Navio turns that uncertainty into a structured path by combining career discovery, personalized planning, skills assessment, task tracking, and AI-guided support in one clean experience.

---

## Overview

Navio helps students move from:

> “I don’t know what I want to do.”

to:

> “I have a direction, a plan, and a next step.”

The app is built for students who want a clearer path toward a future career, whether they are in high school, university, or just beginning to explore different fields.

---

## Key Features

### Career Finder

Navio helps students discover career directions based on their interests, academic stage, preferred working style, and areas of curiosity.

Students can explore possible paths and choose a career direction that feels aligned with who they are and what they want to build toward.

---

### Personalized Roadmap

Once a student selects a career direction, Navio creates a structured roadmap with practical steps.

The roadmap can include:

* Learning milestones
* Suggested skills to build
* Career preparation steps
* Helpful resources
* Portfolio-building ideas
* Experience and project suggestions

This gives students a clear plan instead of a vague goal.

---

### Skills Assessment

Navio includes a career-specific skills assessment that helps students understand their current strengths and improvement areas.

Students answer simple questions, and Navio scores important skills related to their selected career.

Examples of skill areas include:

* Communication
* Data Literacy
* Project Planning
* Programming
* Research
* Problem Solving
* Leadership
* Portfolio Building

The goal is not to judge the student, but to help them see where they are starting from and what they can improve next.

---

### Task Tracker

Navio turns career planning into action by generating practical tasks.

Students can track what they have completed and what still needs attention.

Tasks may include things like:

* Save one useful resource
* Complete a beginner lesson
* Compare related programs
* Build a small project
* Find experience opportunities
* Update a resume draft

This helps students build momentum one step at a time.

---

### Portfolio Dashboard

The Portfolio section brings the student’s career journey together in one place.

It can show:

* Current career direction
* Roadmap progress
* Remaining tasks
* Skills score
* Resume tools
* Personal profile information

This gives students a simple overview of their progress.

---

### Resume Builder

Navio includes a resume-building tool that helps students create a clean starter resume.

Students can add:

* Name and headline
* Contact information
* Summary
* Skills
* Experience
* Education
* Achievements
* Languages
* References

The resume can be previewed and exported as a PDF.

---

### AI-Guided Support

Navio is designed to support AI-powered guidance so students can ask questions, get next-step suggestions, and receive career-related help in a more personal way.

Examples:

* “How can I improve project planning?”
* “What should I do next for this career?”
* “What skills should I build first?”
* “How do I turn this interest into a career path?”

## Purpose

The purpose of Navio is to make career planning less overwhelming for students.

Instead of giving students a long list of random career options, Navio helps them:

1. Choose a direction
2. Understand what that path requires
3. Build relevant skills
4. Take small practical steps
5. Track their growth over time

Navio is built around one simple idea:

> A student does not need to know everything right away. They just need a clearer next step.
