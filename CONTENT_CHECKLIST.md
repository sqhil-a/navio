## Content publishing rule

Before changing an “in development,” “planned,” or empty state to active:

1. Obtain the real facts and links from the accountable organization owner.
2. Verify dates, time zones, eligibility, capacity, cost, accessibility, safety, and consent.
3. Obtain publication permission for every name, logo, image, quote, and statistic.
4. Update the generator, rebuild, run `node scripts/check_public_site.mjs`, and perform manual QA.
5. Record the approver and review date in the organization's internal content log.
