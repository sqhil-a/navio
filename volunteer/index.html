Volunteer

Contribute with purpose, clarity, and care.

Volunteers may help Navio Pathways create resources, support outreach, assist with events, and contribute professional perspective. Roles depend on real organizational needs and appropriate youth-safety practices.

Express interestRead youth safety
No guaranteed placements: Navio Pathways is not currently advertising a specific open volunteer role. An interest inquiry does not guarantee acceptance, a start date, or any number of volunteer hours.

Possible ways to help

Roles will be opened only when scope and supervision are clear.

Resource support

Help research, review, organize, or improve youth-friendly career and skill-building materials.

Outreach and communications

Support mission-aligned community awareness, content preparation, or stakeholder communication.

Event support

Assist with planning, setup, participant communication, or follow-up for a verified event.

Professional contribution

Share career perspective through a structured talk, panel, resource review, or future mentor role.

Youth project work

Contribute to a defined team project with agreed tasks, timelines, communication, and support.

Accessibility review

Help identify barriers in resources, activities, or digital experiences through relevant knowledge or lived experience.

What we expect

Reliability matters as much as enthusiasm.

  • Respect youth, families, volunteers, staff, and partners
  • Communicate honestly and respond within agreed timelines
  • Stay within the role’s scope and ask when uncertain
  • Protect personal information and confidentiality
  • Follow adult-youth boundaries and safety procedures
  • Report concerns promptly through the stated channel
  • Complete only hours and tasks that can be verified

Eligibility and screening

Requirements depend on the role.

Future listings will state minimum age, location, skills, availability, and whether parental or guardian consent is required. Roles involving direct contact with youth may require interviews, references, training, identity verification, or a vulnerable sector check where appropriate.

Navio Pathways may decline or end a volunteer placement when requirements are not met, the role is no longer needed, or safety and conduct expectations are not followed.

Application process

From interest to a defined role.

  1. 1

    Read

    Review the role, eligibility, time commitment, and safety expectations.

  2. 2

    Express interest

    Share relevant experience and what type of contribution you are considering.

  3. 3

    Discuss fit

    If a suitable role exists, Navio Pathways may contact you about next steps or screening.

  4. 4

    Confirm scope

    Begin only after responsibilities, supervision, timing, and documentation are agreed.

Volunteer-hour policy

Accurate records, no blanket promises.

Navio Pathways may document verified service after assigned work is completed and confirmed. We do not guarantee acceptance, a minimum number of hours, or that a role will satisfy a school’s requirements.

Students should ask their school which activities qualify, what approval is needed before starting, and what documentation must be submitted.
