Privacy Policy

How this website is designed to handle personal information.

This draft explains the information Navio Pathways may receive through its website and communications, why it is used, and the choices available to visitors.

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Draft for organizational review: This document is a practical website draft, not legal advice. Navio Pathways must review and approve it before relying on it as a final policy.

1. Scope

This draft applies to information handled through naviopathways.com, website inquiry forms, email communications, and related digital services used by Navio Pathways.

2. Information we may collect

Depending on how you interact with us, information may include your name, email address, organization, role, age range, inquiry topic, area of interest, consent choice, and the content of a message you send. Technical service logs may include information such as browser type, device type, approximate location derived from an IP address, referring page, and request timing.

Please do not submit sensitive health, education-record, government-identifier, financial, or other highly private information through a general website form.

3. Why information is used

  • Respond to questions and requests
  • Assess volunteer, mentor, program, or partnership interest
  • Operate, secure, troubleshoot, and improve the website
  • Send updates when a person has actively consented
  • Meet legal, safety, recordkeeping, or organizational obligations

4. Forms and email

The website form integration is configured separately from the public code. A form must not display a success state unless the receiving service confirms the submission. Email messages are handled by the organization’s email provider and may be retained as part of correspondence records.

5. Analytics and cookies

Analytics is not enabled in the public repository unless a valid analytics identifier is configured. If analytics is enabled, this policy must be updated to name the provider, describe the data collected, explain the lawful and consent basis used, and provide any required controls. Essential storage may be used for security, form operation, or accessibility preferences. The site should not use advertising cookies by default.

6. Third-party services and links

Website hosting, form processing, email delivery, analytics, spam prevention, and social platforms may process information under their own terms and privacy notices. External links are identified where useful. Navio Pathways is not responsible for the privacy practices of unrelated third-party websites.

7. Youth and parental information

Navio Pathways serves young people and aims to collect only information that is appropriate for the stated purpose. Future activities must explain age eligibility, parental or guardian consent requirements, and any additional privacy practices before information is collected. General website forms should not be used to submit confidential school or family records.

8. Sharing

Personal information should be shared only with people or service providers who need it for the stated purpose, or when required for safety, legal compliance, or protection of rights. Navio Pathways does not state that it sells personal information.

9. Retention and security

Information should be kept only as long as reasonably needed for the purpose for which it was collected, required records, dispute resolution, or legal obligations, then securely deleted or anonymized where appropriate. Reasonable administrative and technical safeguards should be used, but no online transmission or storage system can be guaranteed completely secure.

10. Your choices and requests

You may ask about personal information you submitted, request a correction, withdraw consent for future communications, or ask for deletion where appropriate and legally permitted. Identity may need to be verified before a request is completed.

11. Contact

For a privacy question or request, email hello@naviopathways.com with “Privacy inquiry” in the subject line. Navio Pathways must designate and publish an appropriate privacy contact before final approval of this policy.

12. Changes

Material updates should be posted on this page with a revised “last updated” date. Where appropriate, additional notice should be provided.
