Common questions

Before you express interest.

Does expressing interest guarantee a place?

No. An inquiry helps us understand interest but is not an application, acceptance, registration, internship, job offer, or volunteer placement.

Will a volunteer role count toward school requirements?

Navio Pathways does not guarantee volunteer hours. Students are responsible for confirming their school’s eligibility and documentation requirements before beginning any role.

Are programs free?

No fee information is currently published. Each future listing will state the cost, if any, before someone applies or registers.

Can a parent, school, or organization ask about an opportunity?

Yes. Use the contact page and choose the inquiry type that best matches your question.
