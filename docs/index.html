Our mission

Career planning should feel understandable, not intimidating.

Navio Pathways exists to make career exploration more accessible for students who are still figuring out who they are, what they enjoy, and where they might fit in the world of work. It is led by a team of passionate students who care deeply about making that process feel more human, practical, and encouraging.

Explore options

Help students discover career paths that connect with their interests, values, and current goals.

Build a plan

Turn big future ideas into roadmaps with practical milestones, resources, and approachable actions.

Keep moving

Encourage steady progress through tasks, reflection, and skill-building instead of one-time career advice.

Founder

Started by students who wanted career guidance to feel closer.

Navio Pathways was founded by Sahil Ambegaonkar after seeing how often students are expected to make big career decisions without enough clarity, structure, or encouragement.

Today, the organization is led by a passionate team of students working to build tools and resources that meet students where they are, then help them take one clear step at a time.

The Navio app

One project inside the broader mission.

The Navio app is a student career-planning tool being built to help users choose a path, view a roadmap, assess skills, manage tasks, and use AI-guided prompts for career reflection.
